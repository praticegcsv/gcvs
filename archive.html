The Crew Glossary

A translation guide for words we made up or overused.

"Pulling a Marcus" (verb)

To completely vanish from a party or hang out only to reappear 30 minutes later holding a stray cat or random snacks.

"The Danger Hour" (noun)

The time window between 2:00 AM and 3:00 AM when the group starts online shopping for things they absolutely do not need.
